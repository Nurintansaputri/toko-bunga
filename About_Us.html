Profil Floréa

Floréa is a floral studio dedicated to transforming emotions into beautifully crafted flower arrangements. Founded from a deep appreciation for beauty, detail, and the art of expression, Floréa believes that flowers are more than just decorations—they are a language of the heart.
Every bouquet and floral piece we design carries meaning: a celebration, a message of love, a gesture of comfort, or a moment of joy. With thoughtful selection of fresh blooms, refined color harmony, and a gentle artistic touch, Floréa creates arrangements that feel personal, elegant, and sincere.
We are here to accompany your most precious moments—whether big or small—because every memory deserves to bloom beautifully. Floréa — Where every flower speaks.

Visi & Misi

Visi:To become a floral studio that inspires meaningful connections through elegant, heartfelt, and artful flower arrangements.

Misi:
1. Create floral designs with care and artistry to express emotions and stories.
2. Provide fresh, high-quality flowers that enhance the beauty and message of every arrangement.
3. Offer personalized service to help customers find arrangements that truly represent what they feel.
4. Grow with sincerity, professionalism, and creativity in every collaboration and customer experience.
